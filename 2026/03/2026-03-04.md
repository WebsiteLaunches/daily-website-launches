[Website Launches](https://websitelaunches.com) is a public directory of newly launched websites. Sites are automatically detected, and the community votes to surface the best new launches each day.

### Features

- Daily top 100 rankings
- Community voting system
- Categorized by industry and type
- Automated launch detection
- Discover new websites across AI, SaaS, design, and more

### Don't see your site?

1. Visit [WebsiteLaunches.com](https://websitelaunches.com)
2. Click "Request Site Review"
3. Enter your domain
4. We'll check it against our detection systems
